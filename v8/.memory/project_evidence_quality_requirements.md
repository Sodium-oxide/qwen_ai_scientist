---
name: evidence_quality_requirements
description: Requirements for the quality and coverage of the literature and evidence.
type: project
---

Import at least 15 quality-screened papers. Ensure coverage of each selected sub-hypothesis's evidence window. Exclude or de-prioritize content that only reports model AUC without clinical endpoints, only reports disease associations without treatment strategies, is unrelated to precision medicine, or claims direct patient benefits from animal or cell line studies.
