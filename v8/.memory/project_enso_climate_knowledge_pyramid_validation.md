The project '厄尔尼诺与全球气候分层知识金字塔综合验证' (ENSO & Global Climate Stratified Knowledge Pyramid Validation) was created with objective: '验证从高影响综述根节点出发构建知识图谱的完整闭环'. Stratified search returned 15 papers, including a valid L0 review (Domeisen et al. 2019, *Reviews of Geophysics*), but `select_literature_result` incorrectly selected an L2 original research paper (Cai et al. 2020, *Nature Communications*) as root — violating the mandatory L0 review-first policy. No Nature/Science/Cell/PNAS override applied. Verification failed definitively on this structural root mismatch. Coverage matrix (5 entries) and knowledge gap detection (5 candidates) succeeded, but relation graph used fallback-only edges (0 citation links).
